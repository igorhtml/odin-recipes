KFC Chicken Recipe

Description

KFC’s fried chicken is made with a blend of herbs and spices, coated in seasoned flour for a crispy, flavorful crunch. It’s deep-fried to golden perfection, ensuring a tender and juicy inside.

Ingredients

  • • 3 large eggs
  • • Coarse salt
  • • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • • 3 ½ cups fresh breadcrumbs
  • • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • • 8 thin chicken cutlets (about 1 ½ pounds total)
  • • Lemon wedges, for serving (optional)

Steps
