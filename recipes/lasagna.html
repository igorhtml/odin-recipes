Lasagna Recipe

Description

Bolognese lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese. It’s a comforting and hearty meal, perfect for gatherings and family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 150g mozzarela cheese
  • 200ml milk
  • 50g butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Make Bolognese sauce
    • Sauté onion and garlic in a pan with olive oil until soft.
    • Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Make bechamel sauce
    • In a saucepan, melt butter and whisk in flour. Gradually add milk, stirring until it thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble
    • Layer lasagna noodles, Bolognese sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella in a baking dish. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese on top.
  4. Bake
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes.
